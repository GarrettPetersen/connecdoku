About the Creator

Connecdoku was created by Garrett Petersen as a daily word puzzle game that challenges players to think about word connections and categories. The game combines elements of crossword puzzles, word association, and logic games to create a unique and engaging experience.

I'm passionate about creating fun, accessible games that can be enjoyed by players of all ages and skill levels. Your support and feedback help make Connecdoku better every day!
