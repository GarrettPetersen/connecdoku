Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Connecdoku. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our daily word puzzle game.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Progress: We save your current puzzle state, strikes, and solved categories to help you continue where you left off.
  • Statistics: We track your win/loss record, streaks, and performance statistics to enhance your gaming experience.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to save your game state and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress so you can continue playing later
  • Track your statistics and performance
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience
  • Improve our game functionality

Data Storage

All your game data is stored locally on your device using browser cookies. We do not store any of your information on our servers. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your personal information
  • Your data is automatically deleted when you clear your browser cookies

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may collect information about your visits to our site to provide relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your game data by deleting browser cookies
  • Decline cookies when prompted
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
